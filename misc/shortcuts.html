Misc

Shortcuts

tmux

terminal multiplexer

Panes — splitting

Ctrl+b then %
Split pane vertically (side by side)
Ctrl+b then "
Split pane horizontally (top / bottom)

Panes — navigation

Ctrl+b then ↑ ↓ ← →
Switch to pane in that direction
Ctrl+b then o
Cycle to next pane
Ctrl+b then ;
Switch to last active pane
Ctrl+b then q
Show pane numbers, then type number to jump

Panes — resizing

Ctrl+b then Ctrl+↑ ↓ ← →
Resize pane 1 cell at a time
Ctrl+b then Alt+↑ ↓ ← →
Resize pane 5 cells at a time
Ctrl+b then z
Toggle pane zoom (full-screen focus)

Panes — closing

Ctrl+b then x
Kill current pane confirms
Ctrl+d
Close pane by exiting the shell (no confirm)
